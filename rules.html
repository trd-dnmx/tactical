Community Rules

🤝

Respect All Members

Treat every member with dignity and respect regardless of rank. Harassment, bullying, or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.

📋

Follow the Chain of Command

Respect the hierarchy. Report issues to your direct superior before escalating. Do not bypass the chain of command without good reason.

🎭

Represent DNMX with Honor

When interacting with other clans or communities, you represent DNMX. Maintain professionalism and never bring shame to the organization.

⚖️

Accept Leadership Decisions

Decisions made by leadership are final. If you disagree, voice your concerns respectfully through proper channels, not publicly.

PvP & War Guidelines

⚔️

Follow War Leadership

During wars, follow the designated War Leader's instructions without question. Coordination wins battles, not individual heroics.

🎯

Minimum Combat Engagement

All members are expected to participate in wars when called upon. Consistent absence without notice may result in demotion.

📸

War Screenshots

Take screenshots during wars when possible. These serve as evidence for war logs and help document our operational history.

🚫

Avoid Fake Claims

Never fabricate war results or exaggerate performance. Dishonesty undermines the integrity of the entire organization.

🔔

Ping Responsibility

Respond to war pings promptly. If you cannot attend, notify leadership as early as possible so replacements can be arranged.

Behaviour

💬

Maintain Maturity

Act maturely at all times. Childish behaviour, excessive drama, and attention-seeking will not be tolerated within TACCOM.

🔇

No Toxic Behaviour

Toxicity in any form — whether towards members, allies, or enemies — is strictly prohibited. Compete with honor.

🛑

No Abuse of Power

Higher-ranked members must never abuse their authority. Leadership is a responsibility, not a privilege for personal gain.

Recruitment

📝

Authorized Recruitment Only

Only designated members may recruit on behalf of TACCOM. Unauthorized recruitment may misrepresent the organization.

✅

Vet Before Inviting

Potential recruits should be evaluated for activity, attitude, and willingness to follow rules before being invited.

Loyalty & Security

⚠️

No Information Leaks

Sharing internal DNMX strategies, plans, war tactics, or private discussions with outsiders is considered treason and will result in immediate removal.

🔒

Clan Loyalty

DNMX members must remain loyal to the organization. Joining enemy clans, spying, or working against DNMX interests is grounds for permanent exile.

🛡️

Protect Clan Secrets

War plans, internal conflicts, leadership discussions, and strategic decisions must never be shared outside the organization.

Clan Diplomacy

🤝

Respect Allied Clans

Allies are treated with the same respect as DNMX members. Do not provoke, insult, or engage in conflict with allied organizations.

🕊️

No Unauthorized Negotiations

Only leadership may negotiate alliances, truces, or diplomatic agreements with other clans. Members should not make promises on behalf of DNMX.

Development

📈

Continuous Improvement

All members are expected to continuously improve their skills, knowledge, and contributions to the organization.

🎓

Help Train Others

Experienced members should help newer members improve. Sharing knowledge strengthens the entire organization.

Attendance

📅

Be Active

Members are expected to maintain reasonable activity. Extended inactivity without notice may result in demotion or removal.

📩

Notify Before Absence

If you need to take a break, inform your superiors. Unexplained absences during war periods are taken seriously.

Chat Rules

🚫

No Spam

Avoid excessive messages, repeated text, or flooding channels with unnecessary content.

🔞

No Explicit Language

Keep language clean and appropriate. Excessive profanity or vulgar content is not tolerated.

⛔

No Gore or Disturbing Content

Do not share graphic, violent, or disturbing images or videos in any channel.

🖼️

No Offensive GIFs

GIFs and images must not contain offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate content.

🔗

No Link Spam

Do not spam links, advertisements, or self-promotions without permission from leadership.

Doxxing Policy

🚨

Zero Tolerance for Doxxing

Sharing, threatening to share, or attempting to obtain anyone's personal information (real name, address, phone number, photos, school, workplace, etc.) is an immediate and permanent ban from DNMX with no exceptions. This applies to members, allies, and enemies alike.

📢

Report Doxxing Immediately

If you witness or receive any doxxing threats, report them to leadership immediately. Your safety and privacy are a top priority.
